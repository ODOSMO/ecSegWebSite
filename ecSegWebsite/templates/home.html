EXTRACHROMOSOMAL DNA SEGMENTATION

This software quantifies extrachromosomal DNA. It is a systematic image analysis tool that quantifies extrachromosomal DNA with algorithms and artificial intelligence models called convolutional neural networks (CNN). This CNN algorithm is utilized to quantify extrachromosomal DNA-based amplification at the single-cell level, potentially providing new insight into how cell to cell variability concerning specific oncogenes contribute to tumor growth, progression, and drug resistance.
